# Privacy Policy for Smart File Toolbox

_Last updated: July 12, 2026_

Smart File Toolbox ("the app") is an offline, on-device file utility app. This
policy explains what data the app accesses and, just as importantly, what it
does not do with it.

## Summary

**Smart File Toolbox does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal
data.** The app has no backend server, no user accounts, no analytics, and no
third-party SDKs. Every feature — image compression, PDF tools, ZIP
tools, QR codes, and storage analysis — runs entirely on your device. Nothing
you open, create, or scan in this app ever leaves your device.

## Permissions the app requests, and why

| Permission | Why it's needed | What happens to the data |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Camera | To scan QR codes in the QR Scanner tool | The camera feed is only used to detect a QR code on-screen in real time. No photo or video is captured, saved, or transmitted. |
| Photos and videos (media library) | To let you pick photos for the image tools, to save edited photos back to your gallery, and to power the Storage Analyzer, Large Files Finder, and Duplicate Finder tools, which need to read file names and sizes across your library to report storage usage | Files are read and processed locally to perform the action you requested (e.g. compress, resize, convert, or measure size). Nothing is uploaded anywhere. |
| Storage / files (app-scoped) | To let you pick files to zip, unzip, or convert, and to save the results | Files you explicitly choose are processed and saved back to your device only. |

The app does not request location, contacts, microphone, SMS, call logs, or
any other permission beyond the ones listed above.

## Data we do not collect

- No account creation, login, or user identifiers of any kind.
- No analytics, crash reporting, or usage tracking.
- No advertising SDKs or ad identifiers.
- No network requests of any kind — the app functions fully offline and has
  no server component to send data to.
- No cookies (the app is not a website).

## Children's privacy

Smart File Toolbox does not knowingly collect any information from anyone,
including children, because it does not collect information from anyone at
all. The app is safe for a general audience.

## Data retention and deletion

Because no data is ever collected or transmitted off your device, there is
nothing for us to retain or delete on your behalf. Files you create or import
using the app remain on your device under your control, and can be removed
at any time using the app's own File Browser or File Cleaner tools, or
through your device's standard app storage settings.

## Changes to this policy

If this app ever changes to add functionality that involves collecting or
transmitting data, this policy will be updated first, and the update will be
reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
